Main Problem Statement

Ethical AI-driven Career Guidance Platform. Description: Develop an AI-powered career guidance system accessible across India, integrating meticulously designed ethical AI algorithms. Create an interactive web-based platform or mobile application that employs AI models to analyze individual skills, interests, and emerging industry trends. Emphasize ethical considerations in data privacy, algorithmic fairness, and unbiased recommendations to ensure students from diverse backgrounds receive equitable and inclusive career suggestions. The goal is to empower students to make informed decisions about higher education and career paths, fostering a fair, transparent, and ethically sound learning journey for all.
